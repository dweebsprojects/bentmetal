Justin Hanrahan

Owner & Operator

Justin has spent over a decade working in Alberta's exterior trades. He got his start in roofing, learning the ins and outs of how homes are built to handle the province's tough conditions. From there he moved into gutters, soffit, and fascia — eventually taking on work as a subcontractor for established companies across Calgary and Southern Alberta.

That combination of roofing knowledge and hands-on gutter experience is what sets Bent Metal apart. Justin understands how your entire roofline works together, not just the pieces he's installing.

Today, Justin continues to take on subcontract work while building Bent Metal Exteriors into a company homeowners can count on. He's on every job site, involved in every install, and personally stands behind the quality of the work.

Built on Hard Work

If you've lived through a Calgary winter, you know how brutal the conditions can be. Most exterior work shuts down when the temperature drops, but Justin doesn't wait for spring. When the weather gives even a short window and conditions are safe, he's out there getting the job done.

That kind of dedication is what Bent Metal Exteriors is built on. No shortcuts, no excuses, just honest work from someone who genuinely cares about doing things right.
